Intro

I grew up in Spokane Washington and went to high school at Gonzaga Prep. I am currently attending Eastern Washington University where I am studying Visual Communication Design (VCD). When I first started at Eastern I studied elementary education, I changed majors after hearing about the Visual Communication Design program. I am also taking class for a web design. I also found a love for photography when I changed majors. I knew that having skills of designing and making a website would be very handy if I wanted to pursue a career in photography. When taking VCD classes I also found that I loved designing as well.
